## Blog Post Idea: "Any Development Pattern Is Better Than Two"

**Source:** Jack Danger's "Technical Coherence" article (https://jackdanger.com/technical-coherence)

**Core concept:** The cost of having two ways to do something (two ORMs, two auth systems, two deployment patterns) almost always exceeds the cost of the "wrong" single choice. Decision fatigue, onboarding friction, maintenance burden, and tribal knowledge fragmentation compound over time.

### Best Practices Applied

- **Weekly theme** aligned with top-performing blog posts
- **Content spread** across Mon/Tue/Wed prevents audience fatigue
- **Voice:** Direct, authoritative, specific numbers, no emojis, no hedging
- **LinkedIn:** First 100-140 chars must work standalone (mobile truncation)
- **Twitter:** Links in main posts (future: consider reply-based links for better reach)
- **Dev.to:** Full articles with canonical URL to alexmayhew.dev

### Research Notes (January 2026)

- **LinkedIn:** Carousels get 6.6% engagement; optimal length 1,200-1,800 chars
- **Twitter:** External links reduce reach for non-Premium accounts; threads with images perform best
- **Dev.to:** Wednesday 2-6 PM UTC is peak, but Monday maintains better content rhythm
